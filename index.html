Our History

Indian Railways has a rich and storied history that dates back to the mid-19th century. It has played a crucial role in connecting people and fostering economic development across the country.

From the first passenger train that ran from Mumbai to Thane in 1853 to the modern, high-speed trains of today, Indian Railways has constantly evolved to meet the changing needs of the nation.

Explore our journey through time and discover the milestones that have shaped our nation's lifeline.
